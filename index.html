Welcome to Duncannon FC

About Duncannon FC

Duncannon Fc is a small Amateur Soccer team south of Wexford, in Duncannon.
It was Established in 1976. It competes in the Wexford Mens 2nd Division League.
It has Underage teams from under 9 right through to the Mens team. They play their games in Nolan Park in Duncannon.

Duncannon Was founded in 1976 in the village of Duncannon, when younger players of the New Ross Celtic team decided branch off and form their own Club.



Honors

Since Its formation, the club has enjoyed some big honors, winning numourous league titles over the years and even winning the Premier Division of Wexford.


Youth

Duncannon Fc has a long standing tradition of growing and nurturing younger players from under 9s, so that the hard work put into coaching these young people will pay dividends when they reach the Mens Team

Current State Of CLub

The club is Doing Well. There Was a tricky patch some years ago and the Mens Team dropped down the divisions. But They have been doing well the past years, moving up two divisions in Three years. There Is now a drive to raise money for an Astroturf Pitch, and fundraising is going ahead for that.
